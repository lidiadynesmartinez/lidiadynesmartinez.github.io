ACRUI

Sustainable razor and system design

In today’s society, overconsumption and planned obsolescence is becoming a huge drain on the world’s finite resources. Fast moving consumer goods (FMCG) including personal care products such as razors are designed with a linear economic model in mind with over one million razors being disposed of and ending up in landfill sites in the USA alone.

 

This project focuses on designing a razor which delivers the same functionality as current razors in the market while improving its sustainability footprint. In order to protect the earth’s resources and the opportunities of future generations, this linear model must be converted to a circular one, where old products or materials can be reused in some way.
